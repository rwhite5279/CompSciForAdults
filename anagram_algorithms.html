Anagram Algorithms

Identifying whether two different strings of characters are anagrams of each other can be tricky.

Are these two strings anagrams of each other?

tapbgwbtrkoauetgntcqgbuwotrkrstjnyajnvnfvbazmrtxon nqxpytvbtnzbmktuanojwtcekbfrotnguwajtrrvtsgoanrabg

To answer the anagram question, here are a couple of strategies (algorithms!) that you might consider using:

Algorithm #1

  1. As I cross off letters on one list, I'll go through the other list and cross off the same letter when I find it.
  2. If I ever get to the point where I can't cross off a letter from both lists, I'll know that the words aren't anagrams.

Algorithm #2

  1. Alphabetize all the letters in the firstWord.
  2. Alphabetize all the letters in the secondWord.
  3. Compare the two alphabetized lists of letters. If they’re the same, they’re anagrams. Otherwise, they aren’t!
